## Why Manao?

<CardGrid stagger>
  <Card title="Music Commands" icon="star">
    Let your viewers request songs directly from chat. Comes with a built-in music overlay supporting Twitch, Kick, and YouTube!
  </Card>

  <Card title="Overlays" icon="comment">
    Chat and music overlays with multiple themes, fully customizable through the Overlay Settings page in the dashboard.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Soundboard" icon="rss">
    Trigger sound effects instantly, or let viewers redeem them with Twitch Channel Points.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Custom Commands" icon="setting">
    Add your own commands written in JavaScript — they work across all platforms at once.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Open Source" icon="open-book">
    Manao is free and open source. Secure, flexible, and highly customizable.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Multi-Platform" icon="discord">
    Supports Twitch, Kick, Discord, and YouTube in a single bot. Cross-platform account linking included.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
